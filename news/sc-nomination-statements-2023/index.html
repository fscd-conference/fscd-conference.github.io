Steering Committee Membership Election 2023 - ELECTION STATEMENTS

July 2, 2023, 9:04 pm

The FSCD Steering Committee has received two nominations for the election of two new SC members at the General Meeting of FSCD 2023 on 5th July 2023. Their names and election statements are available on the web pages of FSCD 2023. Information about the current composition of the SC is available here. Since the number of nominations is identical to the number of places to be filled on the SC, both candidates will automatically join the SC without an explicit election.
